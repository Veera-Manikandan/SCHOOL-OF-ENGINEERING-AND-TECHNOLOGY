|About us

The Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) has been established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019, located in Tiruchirappali, Tamil Nadu, India. Uniqueness of DSU lies in its multi-disciplinary nature in offering a wide range of academic programmes encompassing medicine and engineering.

|Academics

Academics of DSU is, essentially multidisciplinary, offering major streams of Engineering & Technology stream is offering host of eleven Engineering programs starting from B.Tech Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering to cutting edge branches like B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Sciences to Cyber Security.

|Mission

Its mission emphasizes fostering creative thinking, ethical values, experiential learning, and societal responsibility. The university aims to nurture students with strong language and soft skills, encourage innovation and entrepreneurship, and promote excellence in research and community service. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a green campus, DSU strives to provide an environment where students can reach their full potential and secure successful placements
